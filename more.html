Veggie Vault is a premier organic produce distribution company founded in 2012 by Marcus Langford, a passionate advocate for sustainable farming and healthy living. Headquartered in Portland, Oregon, Veggie Vault specializes in sourcing and delivering high-quality, farm-fresh vegetables, fruits, and plant-based products to grocery stores, restaurants, and meal prep services across the Pacific Northwest. Marcus, a former chef and agricultural researcher, launched Veggie Vault with a vision to bridge the gap between small-scale organic farmers and urban markets. Under his leadership, the company has built strong partnerships with over 150 independent farms, ensuring fair-trade practices and minimizing food waste through efficient supply chain logistics. Veggie Vault is known for its "Harvest-to-Door" program, which guarantees that produce is delivered within 48 hours of being harvested. The company also prides itself on being 100% carbon-neutral, utilizing eco-friendly packaging and electric delivery fleets. With a reputation for quality, reliability, and sustainability, Veggie Vault has grown into a trusted name in the organic food industry, helping redefine how fresh produce reaches consumers.
